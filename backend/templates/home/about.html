Brilliant Academy

About Us

“विद्यां ददाति विनयं,
विनयाद् याति पात्रताम्।
पात्रत्वात् धनमाप्नोति,
धनात् धर्मं ततः सुखम्॥”

[Education leads to sensibility, sensibility attains character /qualification, from that comes wealth and from wealth (one does) good deeds, from that (comes) joy.]- Hitopdesha
Brilliant Academy, a senior secondary school of Science and Arts, established in 2017, has triumphantly accomplished the task of creating a significant mark among the well-established institutions of the town. With an éclat of 100% result in the last AHSEC Final Examination, the Academy has successfully instilled in its students the ability to discover their individual identity that would not only make them sensible but capable enough to contribute to the larger umbrella of world’s growth and development. With the best teaching brains, we are committed to provide the best assistance possible for the progress of the students. We are bent on exploiting the strengths of each of our students to the hilt and to divert each of their weakness into positive direction instilling a value system. The Academy is situated in Chinatalia Road, Ward No. 8, North Lakhimpur. It is a co-educational Senior Secondary School that follows both English and Assamese as the medium of instruction.

Our Vision

The Academy believes in one of the ancient Indian philosophies on education, “विद्यां ददाति विनयं” (Education leads to sensibility) and therefore, it is committed to create a spark in the already existing power of reception in the tender minds of the children through an education that follows both modern and traditional techniques. Our vision is to materialize the dreams and aspirations of our students by inculcating values and morality.

Our Mission

  • To equip the students with advanced knowledge and latest skills in their chosen discipline.
  • To nurture a civilized spirit of curiosity among the students..
  • To provide value-based education that will mould them into good and responsible citizens.
  • To help in the flourishing of the young talents, both hidden and obvious, acknowledging their individual identity.

Our Aim

The famous and wittiest essayist, Francis Bacon says, “Knowledge is power”.
With an aim for the students to achieve power and exercise it for the betterment of our society, we provide quality education through our young, energetic and experienced teachers.

Why Choose Us

  • In order to promote quality education, committed and qualified team of faculties will guide our students in every aspect.
  • Enabling the students to understand the basic of the core subjects.
  • Enabling the students to acquire the skill of analyzing problems to ensure optimum all-round performance.
  • Providing moral values with a view to combat real life problems
  • A continuous evaluation on the second Saturdays and Sundays and fourth Saturdays and Sundays of each month will strictly be conducted and all the students are bound to appear in these examinations, to the failure of which, a fine of a particular sum for each subject will be imposed.
  • In this cut-throat competitive environment, we will give our best to prepare the students to achieve the unachieved and scale new heights.
  • To excel in various physical and psychological activities, the academy conducts Sports Week.
  • Daily doubt clear sessions apart from regular classes.
  • To ensure special care for our culture and tradition.

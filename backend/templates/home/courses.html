Integrated Science Program
  • Every child is a gift and our Integrated Science Program helps them to flourish. From this Academic Session, we are going to introduce this program that will solely focus on the special preparation for Medical (NEET) and Engineering Entrance Examinations (JEE Mains & Advance). The courses are tactfully designed by our concerned faculty members and are unique and as per the latest examination pattern. We shall provide Subject wise Booklets designed according to the syllabus of the entrance examinations. These booklets will focus on building a strong foundation of knowledge and concepts that will ensure the best preparation possible. For adequate practice in each subject, topic wise questions will be distributed to students which will help them in gaining confidence and command on individual topics. We have transformed exhaustive learning process into fun; concepts are simplified to the ground level to help them in learning throughout their lives.

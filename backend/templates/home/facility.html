College Facilities

  • Sports Events

These events are organized annually where the young minds can discover their talents and not be confined within the wall created by rigorous strain from the syllabus and books. These are the following events that the Academy organizes:

  • Exhibition

    • The academy organizes art and photography exhibitions that showcase the bewildering talents of the young minds, equally stupefying the onlookers. These are some of the glimpses of the exhibition.

    Magazine

      It has been rightly said, “Pen is mightier than the sword”; therefore, in order to preserve the talent and abet the independence that is achieved through words, our institution publishes a magazine with the name, “******” . Our students give their best in pouring their hearts out in various forms like poems, essays, stories and the likes. It channelizes the zeal of the budding talented writers; also, the acknowledgement of their talents motivates them to nurture and condition their skill for betterment. Apart from the students, our teachers also have their immense contribution to the magazine through their pieces of writings and this inculcates a tradition of writing in the students.

    Wall Magazine

      It is a periodical run on a notice board and the students contribute their articles, poems and drawing and compositions. Our institution has three wall magazines, one each in the school campus, boys’ hostel and girls’ hostel.
      Prahelika(School)
      Alokjatri (Boys’ Hostel)

  • Library

    • A library is nothing less than a paradise. Our institution has created a well-stacked library, with books and journals of all kinds that help in broadening the critical bent of minds of our students, newspapers, in Assamese and English, to keep the students abreast with the current affairs and to broaden their world knowledge.

  • Laboratory

    • What’s the use of all the classroom interactions without the practical application in a well-equipped laboratory? The laboratories of our institution are definitely fully-equipped with the latest state and are often maintained. The furnishings and the appliances have enough spares for immediate onsite repairs, ensuring the minimum loss of time for the students.

  • Campus Canteen

    • Food makes us happy, always. Isn’t it? But a clean preparation is what makes a canteen special. Hence, ensuring the hygiene, we have one inside the campus, selling some healthy and tasty food items at a student-friendly reasonable price.

  • Medical Facility

    • Unforeseen health related situations are inevitable. To tackle such situations, our institution provides health care facilities to the students by providing First Aid and other basic facilities round the clock.

  • CCTV

    • How can one ensure a healthy interaction among the teachers and students in a classroom? As a solution, our classrooms have CCTVs installed not only to check on the behaviour of the students but also on whether a teacher is teaching efficiently in the class or not. These surveillance cameras ensure the comfort of the parents who are informed on a regular basis with all the good and bad activities of their children and the actions taken by the authority.

  • Hostel

    • Hostel is a place where students discover themselves out of their comfort zones and learn to live together. Our institution provides hostel accommodation to both boys and girls of the college at separate locations. We provide healthy meals with options for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food items. Clean water supply and electricity for 24 hours are special facilities we provide our boarders with. To avoid chaos and to maintain discipline among hostel boarders we have made a few rules and regulations which are being administered by our faculty members as wardens and hostel supervisors. View Hostel Rules

  • Institution Identity Card

    • The institute will provide Identity Cards with coloured photo to the students which must be worn on all the weekdays. Also, on any formal/informal occasion held by the institute, wearing the identity card is compulsory.

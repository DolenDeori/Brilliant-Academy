About Us

Brilliant Academy, A senior secondary school of Science and Arts, established in 2017, has triumphantly accomplished the task of creating a significant mark among the well-established institutions of the town. With an éclat of 100% result in the last AHSEC Final Examination, the Academy has successfully instilled in its students the ability to discover their individual identity that would not only make them sensible but capable enough to contribute to the larger umbrella of world’s growth and development. With the best teaching brains, we are committed to provide the best assistance possible for the progress of the students. We are bent on exploiting the strengths of each of our students to the hilt and to divert each of their weakness into positive direction instilling a value system.

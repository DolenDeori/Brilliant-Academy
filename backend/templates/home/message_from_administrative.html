Message from Rector

Dr. Momina Khannam

“The spread of civilization may be likened to a fire; first, a feeble spark, next a flickering flame, then a mighty blaze, ever increasing in speed and power.”- Nikola Tesla
Deeply inspired by the thoughts of Nikola Tesla, we started the journey of Brilliant Academy, North Lakhimpur, 2017, ensuring to create a spark in our young minds that would later take the shape of a blazing fireball of enthusiasm and energy to excel in life. Before anything else, I take this opportunity to thank you for showing your valuable interest in our institution. I sincerely express my gratitude for all the hard work and dedication of the authority for the establishment of this academy. Our primary focus is to train our students to become persons with conscience, competence, compassion and commitment. Along with academic excellence where the challenges they face in life with critical and certain skills, we emphasize on fastening humanitarian excellence in our students so that they grow up with empathy and social responsibility as their prime focus. Ensuring the best moral character, our students shall learn to discriminate between good and bad. For the last two years, Brilliant Academy has faithfully pursued these goals with single-minded focus, commitment and dedication. All our students, powerhouse of talent and resources, are nurtured through a proper process of personal accomplishment rather than leaving them like the empty buckets filled with a large amount of information. The college curriculum is designed to facilitate intellectual, effective, moral and spiritual growth of each student through various extra-curricular, co-curricular and curricular activities. To facilitate this, we have made a serious effort to assemble a team of dedicated and enthusiastic faculty members. Our goal is to see the academy excels in all the areas of academic excellence. I take this opportunity as Rector to express my gratitude to Brilliant Academy and wish the best for their future endeavors.

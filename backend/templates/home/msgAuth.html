Message from Principal

Mr. Pradip Gogoi

Dear Parents and Students
It gives me immense joy to present the overview of the experiential learning system of our Academy through this prospectus. A senior secondary school of Science and Arts, Brilliant Academy encourages every child to participate whole-heartedly. Quoting Mandela, I would like to ensure that the education we impart shall not only empower the students for their future endeavors but also shall urge their holistic growth and development. They are encouraged to explore their potentials and achieve their personal best in all the aspects of life. Focusing on the development of their individual identity, the students are challenged and engaged through a lot of learning opportunities as our Academy organizes annual programmers that help in developing zeal in being creative, confident and resilient that will ensure an independent and moral way of living in the long run. I am also very fortunate to work with a dedicated, innovative and caring faculty members who have contributed immensely to the well-being of our institution. Apart from academic qualifications, their constant support and encouragement is evident throughout their service. Working and learning together every single day, we strive to provide our students the best environment possible. Welcoming all to the family of Brilliant Academy, I assure never to fail you in any aspect of education that we promise to impart. I hope this prospectus shall give you a deeper insight to all requirements you seek for. Best wishes and regards.

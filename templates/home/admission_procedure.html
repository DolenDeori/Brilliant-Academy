Procedure For Offline Admission

The Prospectus which contains the prescribed application form for admission can be obtained from the administrative office. No duplicate forms will be established during admission. Kindly refer to the following points for detailed information

  • Free Admission

    • For students scoring above 90% in HSLC Examination (Only for General Science and Arts)
    • For meritorious students belonging to economically backward class, 5% seats are reserved in each streams (Only for General Science and Arts)

  • Documents to be submitted

    • Admit card of the student
    • Caste certificate (if any)
    • Four passport sized photos of the student

  • Additional information

    • Original documents must be produced at the time of selection for admission.
    • Application forms should be completely filled and submitted to the administrative office before the last date as notified by the authority of Academy.
